"Photography is a way of feeling, of touching, of loving. What you have caught on film is captured forever... it remembers little things, long after you have forgotten everything." - Aaron Siskind

More often than not we forget we have limited time. Time for laughs. Time for hugs. Once that time is gone we are left with only the memories that we can grasp from within. Photographs capture the time that we have forget to cherish. I have discovered the importance of time and I have found much joy giving that lost time back to you.
